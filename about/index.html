About Me

Hi There! I'm Cole.

I am a computer science student and aspiring cybersecurity engineer at Vanderbilt University. I am a Sophomore working toward a Bachelor of Science in Computer Science and a Chinese Language Minor. I am a member of Vanderbilt's Naval ROTC Battalion, currently serving as a platoon commander in charge of nine other midshipmen. I play ultimate frisbee for Vanderbilt's Club Ultimate Team (the Armada), am a member of Vanderbilt's largest computer science organization VandyHacks, and a co-founder of the Software Engineering Internship Preparation Club.

I am very interested in all areas of the cybersecurity industry, both offensive and defensive. I currently am independently studying network security, penetration testing, and cybersecurity defensive measures in preparation for the CompTIA Certificate Examinations. I am also independently researching artificial intelligence and the capabilities of intelligent systems at the modernization of society and improving the quality of life for all levels of society.

Outside of school, I am an avid weightlifter and future American Ninja Warrior champion, scenic biker, nature photographer, card magician, escape room artist, and more! I love the outdoors and traveling the world (not without my camera!) to learn more about the beautiful planet we live on.
